FAQ

Questions families usually ask.

Is Geni replacing human care?

No. Geni supports reminders, companionship prompts, phone guidance, and family updates. Care decisions remain with people.

Can families update reminders and routines?

Yes. Approved relatives can add reminders, preferences, names, songs, stories, routines, and comfort cues.

Does this work for seniors who are not tech-savvy?

The experience is designed around simple prompts, patient explanations, voice-friendly support, and family-assisted setup.

Is family information private?

Private profiles should be permission-based, with access limited to approved family members and care partners.
